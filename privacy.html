Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Barrie Web Automation ("we", "us", or "our") operates barriewebautomation.com. This page explains what information we collect when you visit our website or contact us, how we use it, and how we protect it.

What We Collect

We only collect information you give us directly. This includes:

  • Your name, email address, and company name when you fill out our contact form
  • The message or project details you send us
  • Basic analytics data (page visits, general location, browser type) through standard web hosting logs — no personal identifiers

We do not use tracking pixels, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics platforms like Google Analytics.

How We Use Your Information

Information you submit through our contact form is used solely to respond to your inquiry and discuss your project. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with any third parties for marketing purposes.

If we build a website or ongoing service for you, we may retain project-related communications for billing and support purposes.

Contact Form

Our contact form is processed by Web3Forms, a third-party form service. Your submission is forwarded to our business email and stored temporarily by Web3Forms. We do not store form submissions on our own servers. Please review Web3Forms' privacy policy for details on how they handle data.

Cookies

Our website does not use cookies for tracking or advertising. Basic session data may be stored by your browser as a normal part of browsing any website.

Data Retention

We retain contact form inquiries and client communications for as long as they are relevant to our business relationship. If you'd like your information removed, contact us and we'll take care of it promptly.

Your Rights (PIPEDA)

As a Canadian business, we comply with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA). You have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent and request deletion of your data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at James.Walsh@barriewebautomation.com.

Security

Our website uses SSL encryption (HTTPS) to protect data in transit. We take reasonable steps to protect any information you share with us, though no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
