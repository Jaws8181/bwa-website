Tech Terms Explained

Google My Business (now called Google Business Profile) is the listing that shows up when someone searches for your business on Google or Google Maps — your hours, phone number, photos, and reviews. It's free and one of the best ways to get found locally. We set it up and keep it updated for you.

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization — it's what helps your website show up when someone Googles "plumber in Barrie" or "best butcher near me." Basic SEO means your site is set up correctly from day one (page titles, descriptions, fast load times). Ongoing SEO means we keep working on it so you rank higher over time.

SSL is what puts the padlock icon in your browser's address bar and makes your website address start with "https://" instead of "http://". It encrypts the connection between your site and your visitors. Google ranks sites without SSL lower, and some browsers warn visitors that the site isn't safe. It's included in all our plans.

Hosting is what keeps your website online. Think of it like rent for the server where your website lives. We handle hosting as part of your monthly fee — you don't need to set anything up or manage it yourself.
